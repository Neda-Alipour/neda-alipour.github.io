About Me

I’m a backend developer who cares deeply about how systems actually work not just writing code, but building logic that people can rely on. I enjoy solving real problems, especially when accuracy matters. I like working close to the business side of software, where data, transactions, and edge cases aren’t theoretical they affect real users.

I have a background in Electrical Engineering, which shaped the way I think: analytically, systematically, and with attention to detail. After graduating in 2022, I found my real interest in software development particularly backend systems. What I enjoy most about backend work is understanding the full picture: how data flows, how rules are enforced, and how small mistakes can have big consequences. I’m not interested in writing code in isolation; I want to understand why something exists and how it’s used.
